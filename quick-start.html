Founding Builder Program

Quick Start Guide

Everything you need to get from zero to a running project with documents in about five minutes.

⏱ Estimated setup time: 5 minutes
1

Set up your Builder Profile

About 2 minutes

Your profile stores your business name, ABN, licence number, and contact details. Once saved, BIK fills these into every document automatically — you never retype them again.

To set up your profile:

  1. Click the Profile button at the top right of the dashboard.
  2. Enter your business name, ABN, QBCC or state licence number, phone, email, and business address.
  3. Add the name of the person who signs documents (usually the principal or site supervisor).
  4. Click Save profile.
Tip: Your profile is stored on this device only — nothing is sent to a server. If you use BIK on multiple devices, you'll need to set up the profile on each one.
2

Create your first project

About 1 minute

Projects connect all your documents in one place. The dashboard then monitors activity and tells you what needs attention.

  1. Click New Project at the top of the dashboard.
  2. Enter a project name (e.g. "Smith Residence — Ashgrove").
  3. Add the client name, site address, and contract reference if you have them.
  4. Set the status to Active and enter the contract value (excl. GST).
  5. Click Save project.

Your project will now appear on the dashboard with a health indicator. BIK will start tracking activity and alert you to anything that needs attention.

Tip: Don't worry about filling in every field right now. A name and status is enough to get started — you can add more detail later.
3

Generate your first document

About 2 minutes

Start with whichever document type is most useful for your current project. We recommend a Quote for a new job, or a Site Diary if work has already started.

  1. Click on a tool card from the dashboard (e.g. Quote Builder).
  2. Fill in the form fields. Your builder profile will auto-fill where relevant.
  3. Click Generate document — the document appears in the Preview panel on the right.
  4. Review the document. Use Edit document to make any last-minute changes.
  5. Use Save PDF to download it, or Email to open a pre-filled email draft.
AI Writer: Most tools have an AI Writer option. Click the ✦ AI Writer button in the toolbar to set up your API key. Once connected, AI will rewrite your notes into professional, clear language — without inventing facts or changing your meaning.

Which tool should I use when?

A quick reference for the tools you'll use most often on an active project.

💰 Quote Builder
Priced quote with line items, GST, and validity date. Clients can approve with a signature.
Use before any work starts.
📝 Scope of Works
Written definition of exactly what is — and isn't — included. Essential protection in a dispute.
Issue with or before your quote.
📋 Variation Notice
Documents scope changes, cost impact, and time extension. Requires client approval before work proceeds.
Every time scope changes — no exceptions.
📅 Site Diary
Daily record of weather, workforce, works completed, and incidents. Your best protection if a dispute arises later.
Every day on site, or as often as possible.
🧾 Progress Claim
Payment claim with schedule of values. Verify your state's security of payment legislation before issuing.
At each agreed claim milestone.
🔍 Defect Report
Formal record of a defect with severity, location, and recommended action. Creates a paper trail for rectification.
When defects are identified at inspection.
4

Send your first feedback

30 seconds

Look for the Beta button at the bottom right of every page. Use it to report a bug, suggest an improvement, or just tell us what you think. Every piece of feedback goes directly to the product roadmap.

You can also rate the tool, tell us how much time you saved, and whether you'd recommend BIK to other builders. It takes about 30 seconds and it genuinely shapes what gets built next.

Founding Builder commitment: We're asking for one piece of meaningful feedback per week during the Beta. In return, we're committed to responding to every piece of feedback and crediting you when your suggestion lands in the product.
