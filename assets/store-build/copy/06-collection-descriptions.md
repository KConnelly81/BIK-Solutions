## COLLECTION: Dust Barriers

**Collection title:** Dust Barriers

**Short description (for homepage card):**
Zipper door kits, poly sheeting and foam seal tape for proper dust containment.

**Full collection description:**
Dust travels. It gets into every surface, every cavity and every corner — and the only way to stop it is to contain it properly before the work starts. Afterthought dust control costs far more to clean up than the right system put in on day one.

Our dust barrier range covers everything from professional zipper door kits that create a clean temporary access point through your enclosure, to heavy poly sheeting for building containment walls from scratch. Add our foam seal tape to close the gaps, and you've got a proper dust containment system rather than a taped-up garbage bag and a wish.

Used by builders, painters, renderers and restoration contractors across South East Queensland.

**Products in this collection:**
- Zipper door dust barrier kit (single / double)
- Heavy-duty poly dust sheeting 2m × 50m
- Heavy-duty poly dust sheeting 4m × 50m
