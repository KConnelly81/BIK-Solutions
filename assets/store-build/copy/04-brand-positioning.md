# BIK Solutions Shop — Brand Positioning

## Brand Platform

**Company:** BIK Solutions Pty Ltd
**Store name:** BIK Solutions Shop (or "BIK Site Protection")
**Tagline:** Beyond Industry Knowledge
**Brand promise:** Professional site protection products for the people who take the job seriously.

---

## Positioning Statement

For builders, renovators and property managers who need reliable site protection products delivered fast — BIK Solutions Shop offers professional-grade floor protection, surface shields, dust barriers and tape, with the product knowledge and trade experience to back it up.

Unlike generic hardware stores or hard-to-navigate trade suppliers, BIK is builder-focused, easy to buy from, and built by people who have actually used these products on site.

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Direct.** No fluff. Say what the product does and why it matters.
- **Trade-knowledgeable.** Use the language builders and site managers use.
- **Confident without being arrogant.** We know this gear. We've used it.
- **Dry humour is welcome.** Think: "You know what the F stands for. We fix those." That voice carries into the shop too.
- **Australian English throughout.** Colour, not color. Aluminium, not aluminum. Labour, not labor.

---

## Customer Personas

**Primary: The Builder / Project Manager**
- Running commercial or residential builds, GC–BNE corridor
- Buys for multiple sites
- Values: speed, reliability, knowing the product works
- Pain point: Generic hardware products that fail; having to go back to fix damage

**Secondary: The Property Manager / Renovator**
- Managing make-goods, tenant rectification, renovations
- Values: products that are easy to order online, delivered fast
- Pain point: Damage to surfaces, time wasted, having to call around

**Tertiary: The Handyman / Tradie**
- Needs one roll of film or a few boards per job
- Values: good price, delivered to site or office
- Pain point: Bunnings is out of stock; trade suppliers want a phone call

---

## Value Propositions

1. **Trade-grade products, not hardware store seconds** — same gear specified on commercial sites
2. **Buy online now, no account call required** — unlike traditional suppliers
3. **Fast GC–BNE delivery** — same-day and next-day available for SEQ
4. **Builder bundles** — site packs designed for real jobs, not shelf aesthetics
5. **Backed by a trade business** — BIK Solutions has been on the tools; we know what fails
