### Orders & Delivery

**Q: How long will my order take to arrive?**
A: Most orders to the Gold Coast, Brisbane and surrounds arrive within 1–2 business days. Regional QLD is typically 2–4 business days. Interstate delivery is 3–7 business days depending on location. We'll send you a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**
A: Same-day delivery is available for select products on orders placed before 10am to Brisbane metro and Gold Coast areas. Contact us directly on 0434 524 270 to confirm availability before ordering.

**Q: Is delivery free?**
A: Yes — free delivery on all orders over $150 (ex-GST). A flat $15 delivery fee applies to orders under $150. Oversized items (large board packs, bulk rolls) may attract a freight surcharge, which will be shown at checkout.

**Q: Do you deliver Australia-wide?**

A: Yes. A tracking link will be sent to your email once your order is dispatched.

### Products

**Q: Are these products the same as what I'd get from a trade supplier?**
A: Yes. The products we stock are the same construction-grade products specified and used on commercial building sites across Australia. They are not hardware store equivalents.

**Q: How much floor area does one board cover?**
A: Each standard board is 2200 × 1200mm, which covers 2.64m². A 10-sheet pack covers approximately 26m² — enough for a large living area or multiple rooms.

**Q: How long does the self-adhesive film stay on before I should remove it?**
A: Hard floor film and carpet film should be removed within 30 days. Window and glass film can remain for up to 90 days. Always remove before the adhesive fully cures to ensure clean removal.

**Q: Will the film damage my floor when I remove it?**
A: No — all our films use low- or medium-tack adhesives that are designed for clean removal from construction surfaces. If you're applying to a freshly painted or very delicate surface, test a small area first.

**Q: Can I reuse the floor boards?**
A: Yes. Heavy-duty floor boards can be reused across multiple jobs if they're handled with care and not heavily soiled. Some wear is normal with use.

**Q: What's the difference between the 600mm and 1m wide film rolls?**
A: The 1m roll is better for wide-plank timber floors, large-format tiles and open floor areas — fewer joins, faster coverage. The 600mm roll suits corridors, hallways, narrow rooms and anywhere a narrower strip is more practical.

### Returns & Issues

**Q: What if my order arrives damaged?**
A: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo of the damage and your order number. We'll arrange a replacement or refund promptly.

**Q: Can I return products I no longer need?**
A: Unopened products in original condition can be returned within 14 days. Return shipping is at the buyer's cost for change-of-mind returns. See our full Returns Policy for details.

**Q: What if I ordered the wrong product?**
A: Call us immediately — 0434 524 270. If your order hasn't been dispatched, we can swap it. If it has been dispatched, return the original in unopened condition and we'll arrange the right product.
