# BIK Solutions — Competitor Analysis

## Competitor Landscape Overview

The Australian site protection ecommerce market is served by a small number of established suppliers who are primarily B2B / trade-focused, with limited consumer-facing UX, weak content marketing and minimal SEO. This is an opportunity.

---

## Competitor 1: Protecta Group — protecta.com.au / protectagroup.com.au

**Type:** Manufacturer + direct ecommerce
**Market position:** Market leader for boards and dust control

| Attribute | Assessment |
|---|---|
| Pricing | Premium; POA on most products; not transparent online |
| Shipping | National; not clear on website |
| Trust elements | 15+ years, Australian-made, commercial project portfolio |
| Product page quality | Detailed product specs; weak lifestyle imagery; no customer reviews visible |
| Reviews | Not shown on site; strong trade reputation |
| Upsells | Minimal — no bundle offers visible |
| Bundles | Not offered publicly |
| Weaknesses | Poor UX; no transparent pricing; poor content marketing; no lifestyle/in-use photography; complex navigation; no mobile-first design |

**Opportunity:** BIK can win on transparent pricing, builder-focused content, and a much cleaner buying experience.

## Key Differentiation Opportunities for BIK Solutions

1. **Builder-first brand voice** — No competitor speaks directly to builders. BIK's existing builder relationship positioning is a genuine edge.

2. **Transparent pricing** — Protecta and Honcho hide prices; BIK shows them. This alone wins buyers who don't want to call for a quote.

3. **Bundle packs** — No competitor offers site-ready bundles. A "starter site pack" or "punchlist pack" is an immediate conversion win.

4. **Fast QLD shipping** — Leverage BIK's GC–BNE territory expertise. ProTrade already offers same-day Brisbane metro — match or cite this advantage.

5. **Content marketing** — Zero competitors use blog content to capture search intent. "How to protect floors during renovation" is low-competition, high-intent.

6. **Trust and reviews** — No competitor displays customer reviews prominently. Being first to show real reviews in this niche builds authority fast.

7. **Mobile checkout** — The existing sites range from poor to mediocre on mobile. A fast, clean Shopify store wins on UX alone.

8. **Trade accounts** — Offer a simple trade account (email or form) for builders to get bulk pricing. No competitor makes this easy online.
