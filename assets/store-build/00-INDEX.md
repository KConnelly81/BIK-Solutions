## Summary of Key Decisions & Recommendations

### Platform
**Shopify** — Basic plan ($39 AUD/month). Best ecommerce platform for Australian trade product stores. Integrates natively with Afterpay, Google Shopping, and all required analytics tools.

### Recommended Suppliers
| Supplier | For |
|---|---|
| Protecta Group | Boards, dust barriers, commercial-grade |
| Protective Trade Group (ProTrade) | Films, tape, same-day Brisbane dispatch |
| All Preparation Equipment | ZipWall dust containment, QLD-based |
| Honcho Supplies | Window/glass film, door protection |

### Launch Catalogue
- 18 SKUs across 4 categories
- 3 pre-built site bundles
- Estimated gross margins: 48–65%
- All pricing ex-GST; confirm actual wholesale costs before publishing

### Competitive Position
BIK Solutions can win on:
1. Transparent online pricing (competitors hide prices)
2. Builder-specific brand voice (no competitor speaks to builders)
3. Site bundles (no competitor offers these)
4. Fast QLD delivery (leverage ProTrade Brisbane same-day)
5. Genuine trade credibility (backed by BIK's existing trade business)
