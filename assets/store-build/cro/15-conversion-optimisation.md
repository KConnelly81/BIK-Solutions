# BIK Solutions Shop — Conversion Optimisation Recommendations

---

## Homepage Layout

### Recommended section order (top to bottom):

1. **Sticky announcement bar** — "Free delivery on orders over $150 | Fast GC–BNE dispatch" (coral background, cream text)
2. **Sticky nav** — Logo left, Collections dropdown + Trade Packs + About + Contact, "Shop Now" CTA button right
3. **Hero** — Full-width, dark charcoal background, headline, subhead, two CTAs (primary: "Shop all products", secondary: "View site bundles")
4. **Trust bar** — 4 icon + text blocks: Trade-grade products / Fast SEQ delivery / Builder bundles / Easy online ordering
5. **Featured collections** — 4-grid (Floor Protection, Surface Shields, Dust Barriers, Tape & Adhesives) with images and CTAs
6. **Site bundles** — 3 bundles with pricing, savings, CTA
7. **Featured products** — Best-sellers or new arrivals
8. **Social proof** — Reviews/testimonials (add as collected)
9. **About strip** — Brief brand statement + CTA
10. **Newsletter sign-up** — Simple email capture
11. **Footer** — Links, contact, policies

### Key homepage CRO rules:
- Primary CTA above the fold at all times
- Price visible (no "call for pricing" on homepage)
- Trust signals visible without scrolling (announcement bar + trust bar)
- Mobile: hero CTA must be thumb-reachable (bottom third of screen)

## Collection Page Layout

### Layout recommendations:
- **Filters** (left sidebar on desktop, modal on mobile): Category, Price range, Size/width
- **Sort by**: Featured, Price low–high, Price high–low, New arrivals
- **Grid**: 3 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 or 2 on mobile
- **Product cards** must show: Image, title, price (inc. GST toggle), short description, "Add to cart" or "Buy now"
- Show **"Save X%"** badge on bundle products
- **Bundle upsell banner** at top of Floor Protection collection: "Buying for a site? See our site bundles →"

### Collection page CRO rules:
- Always show a price — never "POA" or "Request a quote" for standard stocked items
- Show stock status ("In stock" is a trust signal)
- Use urgency sparingly and honestly ("5 left in stock" only if true)
- Cross-sell between collections via "You might also need" at bottom of collection page

## Product Page Layout

### Recommended product page layout:

**Above the fold:**
- Image gallery (main image + 3–4 thumbnail images)
- Product title (H1)
- Price (large, ex-GST with total inc-GST below)
- Variant selector (size/width/pack size)
- Quantity selector
- **Add to cart button** (coral, full-width on mobile)
- **Buy it now** button (Shopify Pay / Afterpay)
- Short trust bar: ✓ Free delivery over $150 ✓ Fast SEQ dispatch ✓ Easy returns

**Below the fold:**
- Full product description
- Specifications table
- Application / care instructions (collapsed accordion)
- Related products ("Goes well with": tape when viewing floor film; boards when viewing film)
- Reviews section
- FAQ (3–5 common questions for this product)

### Product page CRO rules:
- Show GST both ways ("$89.95 ex-GST / $98.95 inc-GST") — trade buyers want ex-GST
- Always show a buy now option (Afterpay/Zip — many builders use BNPL for materials)
- Product images: include in-use photography as soon as possible (before/after on a site)
- Emphasise coverage: "Covers 100m² — enough for a full house floor" is more compelling than "100m roll"
- Variant pricing: clearly show per-unit saving on packs ("10-sheet pack — $299, saving $50 on singles")

## Cart Improvements

- Show order total, GST breakdown, and delivery estimate in cart
- Show free delivery threshold: "Add $X more for free delivery" progress bar
- Cross-sell in cart: "You might also need: Painters tape" (show 2 products)
- Persistent cart (don't empty cart if user leaves and returns)
- Express checkout options visible (Afterpay, Google Pay, Apple Pay)

---

## Checkout Improvements

- One-page or step-indicator checkout (Shopify default is good)
- Pre-fill ABN field for trade customers (or add ABN field to order notes for invoicing)
- Offer "Add to order notes" for delivery instructions (e.g. "Leave at office reception")
- Show trust badges at checkout: SSL, returns policy link
- Thank you page: include "What happens next" (dispatch timeframe), track order link, and an upsell email sequence trigger

## Email Automation (set up from launch)

1. **Abandoned cart** (1 hour after, then 24 hours): "You left something behind — your floor protection is ready"
2. **Post-purchase** (day 3): "How's your order going? Here are the application tips"
3. **Review request** (7 days after delivery): "How did it go? Leave us a review"
4. **Re-purchase prompt** (45 days): "Time to restock? Your last order was 45 days ago"
5. **Welcome series** (newsletter sign-up): 3-email sequence — brand intro, product guide, trade account offer
