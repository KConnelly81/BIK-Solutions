### Your Rights Under Australian Consumer Law

Our products come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have the goods repaired or replaced if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

---

### Change-of-Mind Returns

We accept returns for change-of-mind within **14 days of delivery**, provided:

- The product is unopened and in its original, undamaged packaging
- The product has not been used
- You have your order number or proof of purchase

**Return shipping:** The cost of return shipping for change-of-mind returns is at the buyer's expense. We recommend using a trackable service as we cannot accept responsibility for returns lost in transit.

**Refund process:** Once the return is received and inspected, we will process a refund to your original payment method within 5 business days. We will notify you by email once the refund has been processed.

**Non-returnable items:** Opened or partially used products cannot be returned for change of mind. Custom or special-order products cannot be returned.

### Damaged or Faulty Products

If you receive a product that is damaged in transit, faulty or not as described:

1. Contact us within **48 hours of delivery** at shop@biksolutions.com.au or 0434 524 270
2. Include your order number and photos of the damage or fault
3. We will arrange a replacement or full refund — your choice
4. Return shipping for faulty products is covered by BIK Solutions

---

### Wrong Product Received

If you receive the wrong product:

1. Contact us immediately at 0434 524 270 or shop@biksolutions.com.au
2. Include your order number and a photo of what was received
3. We will arrange collection of the incorrect item and dispatch of the correct product at no additional cost to you

---

### How to Return

1. Email shop@biksolutions.com.au with your order number and reason for return
2. We will confirm eligibility and provide return instructions
3. Pack the product securely in its original packaging
4. Ship to the return address provided

Do not return products without contacting us first.
