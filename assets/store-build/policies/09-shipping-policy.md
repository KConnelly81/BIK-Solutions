### Same-Day Delivery

Same-day delivery is available for select products to Brisbane metro and Gold Coast areas on orders placed before 10:00am AEST (Monday–Friday). Contact us on 0434 524 270 before ordering to confirm same-day availability for your specific products and location.

---

### Order Processing

- Orders placed Monday–Friday before 2:00pm AEST are typically dispatched the same business day.
- Orders placed after 2:00pm AEST or on weekends/public holidays are dispatched the next business day.
- You will receive an email confirmation when your order is placed and a dispatch notification with tracking details when it leaves us.

---

### Tracking

All orders include tracking. Your tracking number will be emailed to you on dispatch. If you haven't received your tracking details within 2 business days of ordering, please contact us at shop@biksolutions.com.au or call 0434 524 270.

---

### Delivery Address

Please ensure your delivery address is correct at checkout. We are not responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer. If you notice an error after placing your order, contact us immediately — if your order has not yet been dispatched we can correct the address.

---

### Undeliverable Orders

If a delivery cannot be completed due to an incorrect address or no access to the delivery location, the order may be returned to sender. In this case, we will contact you to arrange re-delivery (additional shipping charges may apply).

---

### Freight Damage

If your order arrives visibly damaged, please:
1. Note the damage with the courier before signing
2. Photograph the damaged packaging and products
3. Contact us within 48 hours at shop@biksolutions.com.au with photos and your order number

We will arrange a replacement or refund for confirmed freight damage.
