## Notes on Business Model

Based on the supplier landscape, a **hybrid model** is recommended:
- **Stock fast-movers** in small quantities (films, tape) — high velocity, low storage footprint
- **Drop-ship or order-to-order** for boards and large items (reduce warehousing cost)
- BIK Solutions' existing trade relationships and GC–BNE territory give an advantage in establishing trade accounts quickly

*Assumptions: Wholesale pricing assumed at 35–55% below RRP based on industry norms. Actual margins to be confirmed on account setup.*
