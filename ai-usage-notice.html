AI Usage Notice

Effective date: 15 July 2026  ·  Version 0.9 Beta

The core rule: The AI Writer will never invent facts, figures, names, dates, amounts, or claims not present in what you have written. It is an editor, not an author. You are the source of truth — always.

What the AI Writer does

The AI Writer is a professional writing assistant built into BIK Business Toolkit. It helps you turn rough notes and field language into clear, professional construction documents.

When you click an AI mode button, the text you have written is sent to Anthropic's Claude API, processed, and returned with improvements applied. The result replaces or is shown alongside your original text for review.

What the AI Writer will and will not do

  • Will do: Improve grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
  • Will do: Rewrite your text in more professional or formal Australian construction language.
  • Will do: Strengthen contract-protective language — without changing what you said.
  • Will do: Simplify your text into plain language for client-facing communication.
  • Will not do: Add facts, figures, amounts, dates, names, specifications, or claims you have not already written.
  • Will not do: Provide legal advice or guarantee legal enforceability of any document.
  • Will not do: Fabricate information if it cannot improve the text without doing so — it will return your original text instead.

Available AI writing modes

Mode What it does When to use it
Rewrite Professionally Rewrites in formal Australian construction English. Improves clarity and structure. Default choice for most variation notices, scopes, and formal documents.
Strengthen for Contract Rewrites to be legally precise and builder-protective. Reduces ambiguity. When the document will be used in a dispute, claim, or formal contract context.
Spell & Grammar Corrects errors only. Does not rephrase or restructure. When you are happy with the words but want errors fixed.
Simplify for Client Rewrites in plain language, avoiding construction jargon. Client-facing cover emails, explanatory notes, or descriptions for homeowners.
Formal Version Rewrites in formal business English. Correspondence with head contractors, councils, or insurers.
Plain English Short sentences, active voice, everyday words. When clarity and brevity matter more than formality.

Your API key

To use the AI Writer, you need your own Anthropic API key. This key is stored in your browser only — BIK Solutions never sees it. API usage is billed directly to your Anthropic account. BIK Solutions does not charge separately for AI usage.

You can obtain an API key at console.anthropic.com. See the Support page for setup guidance.

How AI output reaches Anthropic

When you use the AI Writer, your text travels directly from your browser to Anthropic's servers. BIK Solutions is not involved in that request and does not store or process the content. Anthropic's Privacy Policy governs how they handle it.

Your responsibilities

You must review all AI output before including it in a document you send to a client, contractor, or other party. You are responsible for the accuracy and appropriateness of the final document. AI output is a starting point — not a finished, guaranteed-correct product.

Not legal advice

Nothing produced by the AI Writer, including text generated in "Strengthen for Contract" mode, constitutes legal advice. If you need legal advice regarding a construction dispute, contract, or claim, consult a qualified solicitor experienced in construction law.
