What's inside

Four sections. Every check you need.

Designed to be run as a quick walkthrough before any trade starts work. Takes 10 minutes. Saves hours of callbacks.

01

Floor Protection

Verify every floor surface is covered before tools go down.

Hard floors — boards, film, polished concrete
Carpet protection film applied correctly
Stairway treads protected
No film on wet or uncured surfaces
Removal date confirmed and marked
02

Surface Shields

Windows, benchtops and doors — the surfaces that get forgotten.

Window and glass protection film applied
External windows sealed against rain
Benchtops and countertops protected
Door frames and jambs covered
Film removal dates within limits
03

Dust Containment

Check the barrier is sealed before dusty work begins.

Perimeter marked and sheeting cut to size
Floor sealed — 200mm fold taped down
Sheet joins overlapped 300mm and taped
Zipper door fitted at entry point
HVAC vents sealed inside containment zone
04

Access Points

The route from site entry to work area — often the most overlooked.

Entry path to work area protected
Lift floors and walls covered if used
Common corridor protection in place
Trade access route clearly marked
