What we do

The work tradies tend to leave behind

Precise, careful services across South East Queensland. Make-good through to site protection — covered under one call, one team.

Our services

Every service is performed with the same standard: careful, precise, and finished properly.

Make-good & repairs

Bringing spaces back to a clean, lettable or sellable standard. Patching, filling, painting, re-fixing and finishing — the detail work that matters when a property needs to be presented properly. We work to the brief, not the easiest path.

Strip & prep

Careful removal of floor coverings, adhesives, wall linings, fixtures and finishes — leaving the substrate ready for the next trade without damage to adjacent surfaces. Strip-back done right means less rework for everyone.

Site protection

Supply and installation of floor protection, surface shields, dust barriers and temporary access coverings. We protect your site before, during and after works so your finishes arrive in the same condition you left them.

Selective removal

Targeted dismantling and strip-back of specific elements — kitchens, bathrooms, partitions and fit-outs — without disturbing the surrounding structure or finishes. Precise deconstruction when precision is the point.

Handyman work

The finishing details that pull a job together — door hardware, shelving, general repairs, small installations and miscellaneous fixes. If it needs a steady hand and a proper result, this is where we come in.

Area care

Ongoing care and maintenance of common areas, tenancy spaces and commercial environments. Regular or one-off — keeping your space looking the way it should between trades, tenants and inspections.
