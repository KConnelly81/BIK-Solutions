# Product Vision

**Purpose:** Define where BIK Solutions is heading and why it matters.
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-15
**Status:** Active
**Owner:** BIK Solutions Pty Ltd

---

## The One-Line Vision

BIK Solutions is becoming the AI-powered Business Operating System for Australian construction businesses — starting as a trades services company and evolving into the platform every builder, renovator, and trade business depends on daily.

---

## The Problem

Australian construction businesses — from sole traders to mid-size builders — run on a broken stack of paper forms, email threads, WhatsApp messages, and generic tools that don't understand construction. The result:

- Hours lost every week writing quotes, scopes, and variation notices from scratch
- Safety documents (SWMS, toolbox talks) copied and pasted without review
- Payment chasing done manually, invoices sent late, cash flow damaged
- Site diaries forgotten, defect lists mismanaged, handovers delayed
- No consistent documentation trail — which becomes expensive during disputes

**The gap between what gets built and what gets documented properly is where most problems live.**

---

## The Opportunity

The Australian construction industry contributes over $360 billion to GDP annually. It is one of the least digitised industries in the country. Purpose-built, AI-powered tooling for this market is largely absent — most existing tools are generic (Microsoft Office) or complex enterprise systems (Procore, Aconex) that are overkill for the majority of businesses.

There is a clear market for:
1. Simple, fast, trade-specific AI document generation
2. Compliance tools built for Australian regulations (WHS, QBCC, BCA)
3. A platform that grows with the business — from sole trader to 20-person outfit

---

## The Phased Evolution

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Now)
BIK Solutions is a trusted trades services business (make-good, site protection, strip and prep) with a premium website and a Business Toolkit of free AI tools that build brand authority and an email list.

**Goal:** Establish credibility, build an audience, test demand.

### Phase 2 — Platform (6–12 months)
Launch a freemium SaaS product — the BIK Business Toolkit — with 10 core AI document generation tools. Free tier generates leads; paid tier ($19–29/month) converts users.

**Goal:** First 500 paying subscribers. Prove product-market fit.

### Phase 3 — Expansion (12–24 months)
Grow to 25+ tools covering documents, compliance, HR, finance, and client communication. Introduce team plans. Target builders managing 3–20 staff.

**Goal:** $50K MRR. Recognised as the go-to platform for Australian trade businesses.

### Phase 4 — AI Operating System (24–36 months)
50–100+ tools including autonomous AI agents that handle entire workflows end-to-end: auto-generating SWMS from site photos, sending payment reminders when invoices age, populating site diaries from voice notes.

**Goal:** Category-defining product. Acquisition or Series A target.

---

## Our Unfair Advantage

1. **Industry credibility:** BIK Solutions is already operating in the construction industry — we are not outsiders building for a market we don't understand.
2. **Voice of the customer:** Direct access to real users — property managers, builders, homeowners — who tell us exactly what they need.
3. **Lean execution:** A small team that ships fast, tests quickly, and doesn't over-engineer.
4. **Trust-first brand:** The BIK brand stands for precision and doing things right — which is exactly what business owners want from software handling their compliance and financial documents.

---

## What We Are Not Building

- A full project management suite (Procore exists — we integrate with it, eventually)
- An accounting system (Xero exists — we generate documents that feed into it)
- A generic AI assistant (we are construction-specific, deeply)
- Enterprise software (our market is 1–50 person trade businesses)
