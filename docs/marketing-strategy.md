# Marketing Strategy

**Purpose:** Define go-to-market approach, channels, messaging, and campaigns for BIK Solutions.
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-15
**Status:** Active
**Owner:** BIK Solutions Pty Ltd

---

## Brand Positioning

**Tagline:** Beyond Industry Knowledge

**Core message:**
> BIK Solutions is the platform that treats your construction business like the professional operation it is — giving you the documents, tools, and systems that the big builders have, without the enterprise price tag.

**Tone:** Direct. Confident. Practical. No fluff. Never condescending.

**What we are not:** We are not a tech startup talking down to tradies. We are from the industry.

## Phase 1 Marketing Channels

### 1. Organic Search (SEO)
**Goal:** Rank for high-intent local and trade keywords.
**Priority keywords:**
- "SWMS template Queensland"
- "builder quote template Australia"
- "site diary template construction"
- "toolbox talk template free"
- "progress claim template builder"

**Actions:**
- Resource pages with downloadable templates (gated with email)
- Blog content on construction business topics (Phase 2)
- Local SEO for BIK trades services (Gold Coast, Brisbane)

### 2. Email Marketing
**Goal:** Build a list of 500+ before paid launch.
**Method:**
- Free tool usage requires email signup
- Lead magnets: free template bundles gated by email
- Weekly newsletter: "Builder's Admin Tip of the Week"

**Sequences:**
- Welcome sequence (3 emails): Introduce BIK, show value, first tool tutorial
- Nurture sequence (ongoing): Tips, case studies, new tool announcements
- Pre-launch sequence: Build anticipation, offer early bird pricing

### 3. Facebook / Instagram
**Goal:** Reach Australian trade businesses where they spend time.
**Approach:**
- Share before/after time savings content ("This took 3 hours. Now it takes 3 minutes.")
- Behind-the-scenes of BIK services business (builds trust)
- Free template giveaways to grow email list
- Paid ads targeting: Australian trades, builders, property managers

### 4. LinkedIn
**Goal:** Reach builders, project managers, property developers.
**Approach:**
- Thought leadership content from Steve (BIK founder)
- Case studies and success stories
- Product announcements

### 5. Trade Facebook Groups
**Goal:** Organic reach within highly targeted communities.
**Groups:** Queensland Builders & Contractors, Australian Tradies Network, Property Managers Australia
**Approach:** Genuine participation; share free tools and resources; avoid overt selling.

### 6. Word of Mouth / Referral
**Goal:** Make sharing easy and rewarding.
**Actions (Phase 2):**
- Refer-a-friend program ($10 account credit per referral)
- "Made with BIK Toolkit" footer on generated documents (subtle brand placement)

## Launch Campaign Plan

### Pre-launch (Now → Phase 1 live)
- Build email list via free templates and toolkit pages
- Publish "Coming Soon" roadmap page with waitlist
- Content: "What's coming to BIK Business Toolkit" social posts

### Soft launch (Phase 1 live)
- Email existing list with early access offer
- 30-day launch discount: Pro at $19/month (normal $29)
- "We're live" social posts with tool demos

### Growth phase
- Case study content: "Builder saves 4 hours/week with BIK"
- Referral campaign launch
- SEO content expansion

## Messaging Framework

**Primary value proposition:**
> Stop doing admin at 9pm. BIK Toolkit generates your quotes, SWMS, progress claims, and variation notices in minutes — built for Australian construction.

**Supporting proof points:**
- "Compliant with Australian WHS legislation"
- "Built by people who've worked in construction"
- "Save 8+ hours per week on paperwork"
- "Cancel anytime — no lock-in"

**Objection handling:**
| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "I don't trust AI for legal documents" | "All documents include a review note. AI handles the structure; you handle the sign-off." |
| "I'm not tech-savvy" | "If you can fill in a form, you can use BIK Toolkit. Takes 3 minutes." |
| "Too expensive" | "At $29/month, one saved hour per week more than pays for it." |
| "I already use Word templates" | "Our templates update when regulations change. Yours might not." |
