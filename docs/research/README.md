## Key Facts (Current Knowledge)

- Australian construction industry: ~$360B GDP contribution annually
- Industry is among the least digitised in Australia
- 85%+ of construction businesses have fewer than 20 employees (SME market)
- SWMS is legally required for all high-risk construction work (Safe Work Australia)
- Progress claims governed by Queensland Building and Construction Commission (QBCC) and Security of Payment Act (SOPA)
- QBCC licensing required for most trade work above $3,300 in Queensland
