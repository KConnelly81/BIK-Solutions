## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
**Price:** $0/month
**Purpose:** List-building, brand trust, product-market fit validation
**Includes:**
- 3 AI tools (Site Diary, Toolbox Talk, Payment Reminder Email)
- 5 document generations per month (watermarked)
- Access to free template library
- Email newsletter

**Goal:** Convert to paid within 30–60 days via value demonstration.

---

### Starter — $19/month (or $190/year — 2 months free)
**Purpose:** Low-friction first payment. Solo trades and sole traders.
**Includes:**
- Everything in Free
- 3 AI tools of choice from Phase 1 library
- 20 document generations per month
- PDF export (no watermark)
- Email support

---

### Pro — $29/month (or $290/year — 2 months free)
**Purpose:** Primary tier. Full Phase 1 access. Target for most users.
**Includes:**
- Everything in Starter
- All 10 Phase 1 AI tools
- Unlimited document generations
- PDF export with custom branding (company name/logo)
- Priority email support

---

### Business — $49/month (or $490/year)
**Purpose:** Phase 2 launch tier. Small teams.
**Includes:**
- Everything in Pro
- All Phase 1 + Phase 2 tools (25 tools)
- Up to 3 team members
- Shared document history
- Xero integration (Phase 2)
- Phone support (Aus hours)

---

### Enterprise — $99+/month (custom)
**Purpose:** Phase 3+. Larger builders and construction companies.
**Includes:**
- Everything in Business
- Unlimited team members
- API access for custom integrations
- Custom branding / white label option
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom tool development (scoped separately)

## Pricing Rationale

**Why $29/month for Pro?**

A sole trader or builder's assistant earning $35/hour who saves 2 hours per week on admin saves $280/month. At $29/month, the payback period is less than 1 day of saved time.

Even at the most conservative estimate (30 minutes saved per week), the ROI is still 5:1.

**Why not charge more?**

The construction industry is price-sensitive and sceptical of software. $29 is the price of two coffees per week — a number that reduces the decision to near-zero friction. We raise prices as we demonstrate value.

**Annual discount rationale:**

17% discount (equivalent to 2 months free) is the standard SaaS convention. Annual plans dramatically reduce churn and improve cash flow predictability.

## Conversion Strategy

1. **Free → Starter:** In-app upsell when user hits generation limit. 
2. **Starter → Pro:** After 30 days, show tools they're locked out of. Email sequence showing ROI.
3. **Pro → Business:** When a Pro user invites a team member and hits the limit.
4. **Annual upsell:** Show annual pricing at signup and in subscription management.
