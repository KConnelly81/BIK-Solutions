What we handle for you

Built for builders

The small works that fall between your trades and your handover date — we close that gap efficiently and precisely.

Defect rectification

Defect lists dealt with properly. We work through builder's defects — surface repairs, sealant, patching, paint touch-ups, hardware adjustments — with the precision and speed your post-handover schedule demands. Every item signed off, not skirted around.

Punchlist completion

The items that always get left at the end of a job — we take the punchlist and close it out. Flooring transitions, door hardware, paint touch-ups, trim, silicone, minor carpentry. One team, one invoice, done.

Post-handover small works

Client moves in, then the list arrives. Owners notice things fresh eyes always find. We handle these post-settlement items professionally — protecting your reputation and your client relationship without pulling your team off the next job.

Site subcontract

Need a reliable subcontractor for strip-back, make-good or site prep on your active build? We integrate with your team and your programme — flexible, professional and insured. One less trade to coordinate.
