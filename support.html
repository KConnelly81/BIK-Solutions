BIK Business Toolkit — Version 0.9 Beta

Getting started

1
Open the Dashboard. Go to dashboard.html — this is your home base. All five tools are accessible from here.
2
Set up your Builder Profile. Click Builder Profile in the top right. Enter your business name, ABN, licence number, phone, email, address, and authorised representative. This information will auto-fill across all documents.
3
Open a tool. Click any tool card (e.g. Variation Notice). Fill in the form — required fields are marked with an asterisk (*).
4
Generate your document. Click Generate document. Your document appears in the preview panel on the right.
5
Save as PDF or email. Use the Save PDF button to open the print dialog, or Email to open your email app with a pre-filled message. Select "Save as PDF" in the print dialog.

Setting up the AI Writer

1
Go to console.anthropic.com and create an account or sign in.
2
Navigate to API Keys and create a new key. Copy it — it starts with sk-ant-.
3
In any Toolkit tool, click AI Writer in the top bar. Paste your key into the setup modal and click Save.
4
AI Writer buttons will now appear next to text fields. Click any mode to rewrite that field's content.

Your API key is stored in your browser only — never sent to BIK Solutions. API usage is billed directly to your Anthropic account. See the AI Usage Notice for full details.

Frequently asked questions

My documents disappeared — where did they go?

All documents are stored in your browser's local storage. If you cleared your browser data, switched browsers, used a different device, or cleared site data for this domain, your documents will no longer be accessible.

We recommend downloading a PDF of any document you want to keep permanently. Do not rely on browser storage as your only copy of important documents.

The AI Writer button says "No API key set" — what do I do?

Click the AI Writer button in the tool header to open the setup modal and enter your Anthropic API key. See the "Setting up the AI Writer" section above.

Can I use the Toolkit on mobile?

Yes. The Toolkit is designed to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile. On mobile and tablet, use the Form and Preview tabs to switch between filling in the form and viewing the document. For saving PDFs, desktop or tablet gives the best print results.

How do I save a PDF?

Generate the document, then click Save PDF in the toolbar. This opens your browser's print dialog. In the print dialog, change the destination to "Save as PDF" (the exact wording varies by browser). On Chrome/Edge, choose Destination → Save as PDF. On Safari, click the PDF dropdown at the bottom left.

The document number isn't incrementing correctly

Document numbers are stored in your browser. If you cleared your browser data, the counter resets. If you need a specific document number, you can type it directly into the "Document number" field on the form — it will not override your typed value.

My builder profile isn't auto-filling

Make sure you have saved your profile: click Builder Profile on the Dashboard and click Save profile. Then open a tool — your profile fields should auto-fill when you start a new document. If the fields still don't fill, try clearing the form (click Clear) and checking again.

Is my data sent to BIK Solutions?

No. All data is stored in your browser only. BIK Solutions does not have a backend server that receives, stores, or processes your documents or profile data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use the AI Writer text in a real dispute or legal claim?

AI-assisted text can strengthen the clarity and professionalism of your documents, but it is not a substitute for legal advice. Before relying on any document in a formal dispute, claim, or adjudication, have it reviewed by a qualified construction law solicitor. See the AI Usage Notice and Terms of Use.

Use the Beta button at the bottom right of any page in the toolkit. Select Bug Report, Feature Request, or General Feedback. Add a description and hit Submit — it takes about 30 seconds. A mailto link will open with your feedback pre-filled. Your email client sends it directly to the BIK team.

You can also email us directly at support@biksolutions.com.au. Founding Builders receive a personal reply to every support request.
