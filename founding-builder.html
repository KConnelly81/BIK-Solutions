A note from BIK

Why we built this — and why your feedback matters more than you think.

BIK started with one observation: builders spend hours every week on paperwork that has nothing to do with building. Quotes, variations, site diaries, progress claims — the admin load on small building businesses is genuinely punishing.

Most software built for builders is either too expensive, too complicated, or built by people who've never been on a construction site. We wanted to build something different: fast, practical, Australian, and designed around how builders actually work.

The Beta you're using today is real, functional software — but it's not finished. The tools are complete enough to use on live projects. What's missing is the friction you'll discover by actually using it. The fields that don't make sense. The steps that feel backwards. The document that doesn't quite cover your situation.

That's exactly what we need from you. Not polished feedback — just honest reactions. "This confused me." "I needed X and couldn't find it." "This saved me an hour." Every response goes directly to the product roadmap.

In return, Founding Builders receive early access to every new tool, locked-in pricing when we launch commercially, and the ability to vote on what gets built in each sprint.

How the program works

1

Set up your profile

Enter your business details once. BIK fills them across every document automatically — no retyping on every form.

2

Use BIK on a real project

Create a project, generate a quote, a site diary, a variation. Use the tools the way you'd use them in real life — not in demo conditions.

3

Send feedback when something stands out

Use the feedback button on any page — it takes 30 seconds. A bug, a missing field, a document that printed wrong, a feature you wish existed. All of it helps.

4

Watch your feedback become features

Every sprint we ship improvements based on Founding Builder feedback. You'll see your suggestions land in the product — and we'll tell you when they do.
