BIK Business Toolkit  ·  Updated July 2026

BIK Business Toolkit is being built to replace folders, spreadsheets, and generic software for Australian builders and tradies. Every feature decision is filtered against one question: does this reduce duplicate work, save time, reduce disputes, or protect cash flow? If not, we don't build it.

Product Mission

Every feature must reduce work, save time, reduce disputes, or protect cash flow. BIK is not a document library. It is an intelligent office administrator for builders who do not have one. Workflow over feature count. Connection over isolation. Proactive over reactive.
